Pricing Feels Difficult When The Product Feels Unclear

Many beginners struggle with pricing because they are trying to price a product that still feels unclear. This workshop helps you define who the product helps, what problem it solves, what result it creates and how to communicate that value clearly. Once those pieces are clear, choosing a starting price becomes much easier.

What This Workshop Helps You Do

Build a simple beginner pricing foundation:

  • Define who your product helps
  • Identify the problem it solves
  • Clarify the result it creates
  • Create a positioning statement
  • Choose a realistic starting price

Is This Workshop For You?

It's a YES if...

You feel unsure about how to price your product or explain its value clearly.

It's NOT for you if...

You are looking for advanced pricing psychology or enterprise pricing systems.

What You'll Build Inside

You will complete practical pricing exercises alongside the workshop lessons. By the end, you'll create:

  • A Pricing Sheet
  • An Audience section
  • A Problem Removed section
  • A Result Created section
  • A Positioning Statement
  • A Starting Price

What Makes This Different

This workshop removes the pressure and confusion around pricing. Instead of trying to find the perfect price, you'll build a practical starting point you can improve over time through experience and feedback.
