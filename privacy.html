Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Last updated: 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, purchase information and any messages you submit through forms on this website.

We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device information and website usage data through analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver digital products, provide customer support, respond to inquiries, improve our content and better understand how visitors use our website.

We may also use your information to send educational content, updates, product announcements and other communications related to Kalz Learn.

3. Email Communications

If you download a free resource, subscribe to our email list or purchase a product, you may receive emails from Kalz Learn.

These emails may include educational content, updates, product information and other resources designed to help creators build and sell digital products.

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link included in those emails.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate and improve our business. These services may include payment processors, digital product delivery platforms, analytics providers, email marketing providers and form management tools.

Examples may include Payhip, MailerLite, Google Analytics, Google Sheets and other tools used to support the operation of Kalz Learn.

These services have their own privacy policies and practices, which are outside our control.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, remember preferences and analyze website traffic.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure or loss.

However, no method of transmitting or storing data online can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of or deletion of your personal information where applicable.

You may also unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

If you have questions about your data or how it is used, please contact us through the contact page on this website.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, business practices or legal requirements.

Any updates will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately upon publication.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through the contact page on this website.
